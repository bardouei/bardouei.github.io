About Me

iOS Engineer with 5+ years of experience in building complex, scalable applications. Proven expertise in the full mobile development lifecycle, from architecture to deployment. Skilled in integrating advanced technologies like computer vision to enhance app capabilities, with a strong focus on clean code, testable architecture, and performance optimization. I specialize in utilizing modern Swift frameworks (such as SwiftUI and Combine) and applying rigorous design patterns like MVVM and VIPER to ensure high maintainability. My background includes end-to-end responsibility for CI/CD pipelines, automating build processes, and deploying to the App Store efficiently. I am passionate about TDD/BDD practices and committed to delivering robust, crash-free user experiences. Furthermore, I thrive in collaborative environments, translating complex business requirements into elegant, high-performing mobile solutions.
